It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your mobile device when you use an application. Cookies are widely used by app developers to make their apps work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the app owner (in this case, StrapazziDesignStudio) are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by parties other than the app owner are called "third-party cookies." Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided within the app (e.g., advertising, analytics). The parties that set these third-party cookies can recognize your device both when it uses our App and when it visits certain other apps or websites.

2. Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our App to operate, and we refer to these as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. Other cookies enable us and third parties to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our App. Third parties also serve cookies through our App for advertising, analytics, and other purposes.

5. How Can You Control Cookies?

Mobile Device Settings

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. Most mobile operating systems provide settings that allow you to control or reject cookies, or to alert you when a cookie is being set.

Android devices:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • You can also reset your advertising ID

Opt-Out from Targeted Advertising

You can opt out of targeted advertising by:

  • Using the settings on your mobile device (as described above)
  • Visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page
  • Visiting the European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (for EU users)

Google Analytics Opt-Out

To opt out of being tracked by Google Analytics across all websites and apps, visit: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

⚠️ Important: Please note that if you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our App, but your access to some functionality and areas may be restricted, and some features may not work properly.

6. Cookie Duration

Cookies can be classified by their duration:

  • Session cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close the App
  • Persistent cookies: These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. The expiration time varies depending on the cookie's purpose

7. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some mobile operating systems include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting that can request that an application disable its tracking or cross-site user tracking. At this time, our App does not respond to DNT signals.
